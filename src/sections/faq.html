Do I have to be identified as a woman to participate?

No, at DjangoFest, we welcome participants of all genders.

I don't live in Kansas City, can I attend?

Of course! We have had participants from Omaha and Oklahoma City in prior years.

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch will be provided during workshops.

Anyone aged 14 and up can participate.

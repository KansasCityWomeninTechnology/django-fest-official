DjangoFest

If you want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you: we are holding a free workshop for beginners! It will take place January 27, 2024. Kansas City Women in Technology's ongoing mission is to grow the number of women in technology careers in Kansas City.
KCWiT is excited to introduce people to coding and technology through this Django workshop. We want to give you an opportunity to learn how to program! This workshop is free of charge, and some meals will be provided.

Schedule

January 27th, 2024
  • 8:30 - 9:00 am Attendee check-in
  • 9:00 - 9:15 am Opening presentation & kickoff, light breakfast
  • 9:15 am - 12:30 pm Session
  • 12:30 - 1:30 pm Lunch break
  • 1:30 - 5:00 pm Session
  • 5:00 - 5:30 pm Closing presentation, retrospective

FAQ

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch will be provided during workshops.

Who can attend?

Anyone ages 14 and up.

I don't live in Kansas City, can I attend?

Of course! Groups of women from Omaha and Oklahoma City have joined us in prior years.

Definitely! You will be responsible for lodging and transportation.

About

If you want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you: we are holding a free co-ed workshop for beginners! It will take place January 27, 2024 at Keystone Innovation District. Kansas City Women in Technology's ongoing mission is to foster diversity and inclusion in technology careers in Kansas City.
KCWiT is excited to introduce people from all gender and backgrounds to coding and technology through this Django workshop. We want to give you an opportunity to learn how to program! This workshop is free of charge, and some meals will be provided.

FAQ

Don't see an answer to your question below? Please contact us at djangofest@kcwomenintech.org!

Do I have to be identified as a woman to participate?

No, at DjangoFest, we welcome participants of all genders.

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch will be provided during workshops.

Do I need to know anything about websites or programming?

No! Workshops are for beginners. You donâ€™t need to know anything about it. However, if you have a little bit of technical knowledge (i.e. you know what HTML or CSS are) you still can apply!

I don't live in Kansas City, can I attend?

Of course! We have had participants from Omaha and Oklahoma City in prior years.

What are the age requirements?

Anyone aged 14 and up can participate.

Do I need to have something installed on my laptop?

It would be helpful to have Django installed before workshops, but we wouldn't expect you to install anything on your own.

I do not live in Kansas City. Can I still attend?

Definitely! You will be responsible for lodging and transportation.

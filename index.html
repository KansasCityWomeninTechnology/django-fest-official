About

If you want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you: we are holding a free co-ed workshop for beginners! It will take place October 12, 2024 at H&R Block world headquarters in downtown Kansas City.

Kansas City Women in Technology's ongoing mission is to foster diversity and inclusion in technology careers in Kansas City. We are excited to introduce learners of all genders and backgrounds to software development, also known as “coding” through hands-on, experiential learning.

Thanks to our generous sponsors, this workshop is free of charge, and some meals are provided.

FAQ

Don't see an answer to your question below? Please contact us at djangofest@kcwomenintech.org

Do I have to be identified as a woman to participate?

No, at DjangoFest, we welcome participants of all genders.

I don't live in Kansas City, can I attend?

Of course! We have had participants from Omaha and Oklahoma City in prior years.

Is food provided?

Yes. Thanks to our sponsors, lunch will be provided during workshops.

What are the age requirements?

Anyone aged 14 and up can participate.

Do I need to know anything about websites or programming?

No! Workshops are for beginners. You don’t need to know anything about it. However, if you have a little bit of technical knowledge (i.e. you know what HTML or CSS are) you still can register!

Do I need to have something installed on my laptop?

It would be helpful to have Django installed before workshops, but we wouldn't expect you to install anything on your own.

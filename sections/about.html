About

If you want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you: we are holding a free co-ed workshop for beginners! It will take place October 12, 2024 at H&R Block world headquarters in downtown Kansas City.

Kansas City Women in Technology's ongoing mission is to foster diversity and inclusion in technology careers in Kansas City. We are excited to introduce learners of all genders and backgrounds to software development, also known as “coding” through hands-on, experiential learning.

Thanks to our generous sponsors, this workshop is free of charge, and some meals are provided.
